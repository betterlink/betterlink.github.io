Betterlink - Webmaster Setup

  • Step 1 of 2: Payments

Betterlink uses a simple payments model: you pay when we drive you traffic.

Compared to normal links to your content, Betterlink creates a stronger experience for you and your visitors.

  • Simple user experience
  • Easier to create and find links to your content on-the-fly
  • Increased page engagement for visitors
  • Discover insights on which content drives shares, visits, and engagement

We use a fixed cost-per-click (CPC) model on a monthly payment cycle. The charge: $0.44 per visit.

Discounted rates are available for non-profits or organizations with large volume.

Setting Up Your Payments Profile

For now, initial account setup will be handled through email. Please contact us at account-setup@betterlink.io to start the process.

Once your account is created, you'll receive an account ID and an API Key. You'll need the API Key to authenticate your website once you have integrated with the JavaScript library.

Testing Betterlink Before Account Creation

The good news: you'll be able to test Betterlink on your website before you've fully setup an account. The first 100 visits to your domain or the first 15 days (whichever happens first) is your trial period. Immediately following your trial period, we will start tracking page visits using Betterlink.

All you need to do is setup Betterlink according to the setup steps, and everything will work automatically.

At the end of your trial period, if users continue to visit your custom Betterlink links, they'll receive the same experience as if it were a regular, plain-old hyperlink (so your users won't be negatively impacted).
