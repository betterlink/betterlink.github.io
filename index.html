Betterlink

  • Add one script to your website
  • Enable links directly to the content people want to see
Copy this JavaScript 
                  
    <script>
      (function(d,s,id){
      var js,fjs = d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0];
      if(!d.getElementById(id)){
      js=d.createElement(s); js.id = id; js.defer = true;
      js.src="//code.betterlink.co/betterlink.js";
      js.setAttribute('data-script_source','within source');
      fjs.parentNode.insertBefore(js,fjs);}
      }(document,'script','betterlink-js'));
    </script>

How does this work?

When your webpage loads, this will create a new <script> element to request Betterlink for your site. To make sure your page loads as fast as possible, we use this special format -- this way we don't block any other content from loading!

Additionally, the defer attribute lets your browser know it doesn't need to execute Betterlink until the rest of your page has loaded.

Betterlink documentation

By default, simply adding this one script onto your site will instantly enable Betterlink -- to both share new links and highlight the content of followed links.

Tips on Betterlink placement

There are two things you want to keep in mind when adding Betterlink to your website:

  1. loading your own content as fast as possible
  2. ensuring Betterlink loads on all of the pages on your site

Quick and simple answer: insert the script right before the closing </body> tag on your pages

For more details, reference our documentation on placement.

Browser Support

The short answer: Betterlink is supported by all modern browsers and most older browsers.

IE7+ is officially supported, and IE6 should work (however, it has not been thoroughly tested).

International Support

If there's text that displays on your page, Betterlink will be able to link straight to it. It doesn't matter if you say 'hello' as 你好 or שָׁלוֹם.

Yes, that includes emoji too ☺
