Betterlink - Webmaster Setup

  • Step 2 of 2: Customize

Not all sites are created the same, and we can help yours rise to the top.

Betterlink has a great standard user experience, but we know that sometimes you need to do things your own way. Luckily, Betterlink has a host of features that let you control how your visitors engage with your content.

In order to customize the Betterlink experience, you'll need two things:

  • A Payments Profile for billing
  • An API Key to customize Betterlink during initialization

Customization is a paid feature within Betterlink, though your payments will stay proportional with the value you're getting out of this service. It's simple: you only pay when we successfully drive you traffic.

Customization Options

  • The look and feel of how your page is highlighted
  • Control over dynamic and user-generated content
  • Which content can and can't be linked-to
  • Permalink support
  • How Betterlink stores selections in your URLs

Full details on customizing Betterlink are found in the Advanced Setup documentation.

Setting Up Your Payments Profile

We use a fixed cost-per-click (CPC) model on a monthly payment cycle. The charge: $0.44 per visit.

For now, initial account setup will be handled through email. Please contact us at account-setup@betterlink.io to start the process.

Once your account is created, you'll receive an account ID and an API Key. You'll need the API Key to authenticate your website once you have integrated with the JavaScript library.

In addition, we'll send you details on how to use our customizable betterlink.js library to setup your service.
